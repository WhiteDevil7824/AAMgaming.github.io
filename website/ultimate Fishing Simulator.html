There are two modes of difficulties in the Ultimate Fishing Simulator, thanks

to which there is something for everyone. Normal Mode allows you to enjoy

the game in all its glory. In normal mode, all the features in the game are

included. Realistic Mode requires more patience and efficiency from the

player. Fish will be biting less and will fight longer. Some of the game

features in this mode are disabled, such as the underwater camera.
