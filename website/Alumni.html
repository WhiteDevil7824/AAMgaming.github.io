Alumni Escape Room


Working long hours on your recent research with experimental time-shifting

serums at Ridgeway Space/Time Labs has taken its toll. You promise

yourself a break after the next batch of results.Taking a moment after hours

of painstaking research you drowsily make yourself a refreshing coffee,

moments later some drops of the serum accidentally fall into your mug.



Alumni is a first person 3d escape room puzzle adventure, similar to games

you may have played in the ’90’s or similar to a virtual escape rooms you may

have played. An indoor immersive world that you explore and an inventory

where you can collect and use items you find along the way to solve puzzles

and escape the rooms.



Play as a scientist transported back in time to memories of college fighting to

escape the nightmare reality. Explore areas and take in your surroundings.

Use all of your puzzle solving skills to make your way through the college and

return back to the present.How you tackle the puzzles is down to you. Each

puzzle has a logical solution, so take your time, there is no rush and enjoy the

process of understanding what you have to do.
