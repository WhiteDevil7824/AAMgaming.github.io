Girl Genius: Adventure In Castle


Girl Genius: Adventures In Castle Heterodyne is a platform video game

created by Rain Games of Teslagrad fame, with contributions by the

Professors Foglio. As indicated by the title, the player portrays Agatha

as she enters the Castle and navigates its hazards as she attempts to

repair it, with Zola being her ultimate nemesis. A successful Kickstarter

campaign was completed in the fall of 2020, bringing in $250,000 of the

requested $200,000, and the game was formally released in September of

2023.



The plot only loosely follows that of the comic, with the action involving

numerous never-before-seen clanks that must be battled and defeated.

Along with Agatha and Zola, thanks to achieving some Kickstarter stretch

goals, other characters who appear include Zeetha, Violetta, Othar and

Sanaa, Theo and Sleipnir, and Krosp. Agatha is assisted by at least one

dingbot, and has a chance to wield the infamous Razor of Occam Heterodyne.



Gameplay



Build tools like the Grapple Gun and the Wrencherang at the Workbench,

and use your tools to explore and solve the castle’s challenges.Discover

numerous sights and secrets, by being perceptive and using your tools to the

fullest.Defeat foes in furious melee combat or with explosive gadgets, and

utilize the environment to your advantage using your tools.



Use your trusty Dingbot minion to explore places Agatha can’t reach, by

moving in tight spaces, digging beneath or soaring above danger.Use the

Dingbot to solve lock puzzles, and operate the internal machinery of the

castle.As Agatha’s power over the castle grows, command the castle AI to

release traps at your foes, and activate mechanisms.



Use the unique “Build System”, rebuilding scrap traps into objects like the

Springclank, Grappleclank and the special Rollerclank, giving the player a

suite of tools for creative spatial puzzle solving.Interact with several of the

fantastic characters from “Girl Genius™”, like Gilgamesh Wulfenbach,

Tarvek Sturmvoraus and many others.Read the Girl Genius™ graphic

novels in the game! Note! This feature is in English only.
